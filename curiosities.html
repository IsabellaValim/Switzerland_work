Curiosities

Cultural Facts

Punctuality is a national virtue: Being late for an appointment, even a social one, can be seen as disrespectful
Sunday is sacred (almost literally): It’s common for supermarkets to close, and noisy activities like vacuuming or mowing the lawn are avoided on Sundays.
Every region has its own identity: A Swiss person from the French-speaking canton may have very different habits from one in the German-speaking canton — it’s like "mini-countries" within the country.
Respect for nature: The Swiss take the environment very seriously — recycling is mandatory and extremely organized.
Fondue is a real tradition: And they take it seriously: if your bread falls into the pot, there’s often a funny "penalty" like buying wine or kissing the person next to you!

Historical Facts

he army hasn’t been used in war for centuries, but every Swiss man is trained and serves mandatory military service.
The Red Cross was founded in Geneva in 1863, and its flag is the reverse of the Swiss flag.
The Vatican Swiss Guard: The Pope's guards are Swiss! This tradition began in the 16th century.
Switzerland only joined the United Nations in 2002, despite hosting the UN headquarters in Geneva since its foundation.
The Swiss banking system was famous for absolute secrecy, which only started to change in the 2000s due to international pressure.

Funny / Unusual Facts

Flushing the toilet after 10 p.m. is banned? In some old buildings, yes! It’s considered too noisy and might disturb neighbors.
Horses have health insurance: Large animals like horses can have health insurance — and this is not a joke!
Fines based on income: A millionaire once received a speeding ticket of over 200,000 Swiss francs because fines are proportional to income.
Mountain bank vaults: Secret bunkers hidden in the Swiss Alps store gold, art, and other valuables for millionaires worldwide.
IDifficult citizenship: In some small villages, residents vote on whether you can become a Swiss citizen — they literally ask the neighbors what they think about you.
