Discover the Best of Switzerland

Four Official Languages: Switzerland has four official languages: German, French, Italian, and Romansh. The language spoken depends heavily on the region.

No Fixed President: The country is governed by a Federal Council of seven members. The presidency rotates among them every year — it is more symbolic than executive.

Famous for Chocolate and Cheese: Switzerland is a world reference in chocolate production (like Lindt and Toblerone) and has more than 450 types of cheese.

Precision Watches: Swiss brands like Rolex, Omega, and Patek Philippe are synonymous with quality and luxury. The watchmaking tradition is very strong there.

Historical Neutrality: Switzerland has been neutral in armed conflicts for centuries and did not participate in either of the World Wars.

Punctual Transportation: Swiss trains are known for their extreme punctuality — if a train is delayed by two minutes, it is already considered unusual.

Stunning Landscapes: The Swiss Alps offer breathtaking landscapes and are perfect for skiing, hiking, and panoramic train rides.

High Cost of Living: Cities like Zurich and Geneva are among the most expensive places in the world to live.

Direct Democracy: Swiss citizens can frequently vote in referendums, directly deciding on many laws and constitutional changes.

Swiss Army Knife: The famous multifunctional knife was invented there and is a national symbol.
