Foods

🧀 Fondue

• Description: Melted cheese (usually Gruyère and Emmental) served in a heated pot, where pieces of bread are dipped.
• Accompaniment: White wine and kirsch (cherry liqueur) are often used in its preparation.

🍲 Cheese Soup (Käsesuppe)

• Description: Creamy soup made with Swiss cheese, usually served with bread.

🧀 Raclette

• Description: Melted raclette cheese scraped over boiled potatoes, pickles, pearl onions, and sometimes cured meats.
• Origin: Alpine region.

🥔 Rösti

• Description: A type of pancake made from grated potatoes, fried until golden and crispy.
• Variation: Can be plain or include onion, cheese, bacon, among others.

🍫 Swiss Chocolate

• Description: World-famous, known for its smooth texture and high quality.
• Famous brands: Lindt, Toblerone, Nestlé (founded in Switzerland).

🥩 Zürcher Geschnetzeltes

• Description: Sliced veal in a creamy sauce with white wine and mushrooms.
• Served with: Rösti.

🍞 Birchermüesli

• Description: A cold cereal made with oats, fresh fruits, nuts, and yogurt or milk.
• Creation: Invented by a Swiss doctor as a healthy meal.

🍞 Pain de Seigle (Rye Bread)

• Description: Very common in the French-speaking part of Switzerland, especially in the Valais region.
